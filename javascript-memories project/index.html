How the UN speech by RM changed my life.

On September 24, 2018, Kim Namjoon, also known as RM, stood before the United Nations as the leader of BTS and delivered a speech that not only touched the world but deeply impacted my own life. I can vividly recall watching him speak, his words reaching straight into my heart, igniting emotions and reflections I had long kept buried.

"Speak yourself." These two simple words became the foundation of RM's message, but they carried a powerful meaning that I hadn’t fully grasped until that moment. For years, I struggled with accepting who I truly was. I was afraid of judgment, criticism, and rejection, so I chose to stay silent about my thoughts and feelings. But hearing RM, someone I admired, stand confidently in front of world leaders and youth alike, encouraging everyone to embrace who they are and to speak their truth, planted a seed of change in me.RM’s speech was more than just words— it was an invitation to accept myself, flaws and all.

When he said, “I want to hear your voice, and I want to hear your conviction, no matter who you are, where you're from, your skin color, gender identity—speak yourself,” I felt for the first time that my voice had value. It wasn’t just about being heard by others, but about hearing myself, acknowledging my inner thoughts, dreams, and feelings, and having the courage to express them.

After watching the speech, I made a conscious decision to step out of my comfort zone. I started to pursue my passions without the fear of judgment holding me back. I learned that it was okay to make mistakes, as long as I stayed true to myself. RM’s message helped me realize that vulnerability is not a weakness but a strength, and that self-love begins with accepting and expressing the most authentic version of oneself.Through this journey of self-acceptance, I’ve grown into a more confident person.

RM’s words gave me the strength to chase after my dreams, unapologetically. Whether it's in my relationships, career choices, or creative pursuits, I’ve learned to speak myself and be proud of the person I am becoming."No matter who you are, where you’re from, your skin color, gender identity, just speak yourself. " These words have been my guiding light ever since.

They remind me that my voice matters, that I matter, and that I deserve to live a life that reflects my true self. RM’s UN speech didn’t just inspire me; it transformed me, and for that, I will forever be grateful.

My favorite Album

The Most Beautiful Moment in Life, Pt. 1," released in 2015, holds a special place in my heart as my favorite BTS album. This album beautifully encapsulates the essence of youth, capturing the highs and lows of growing up. Each track resonates with personal experiences, making it relatable and impactful.One of the standout aspects of this album is its exploration of themes like love, loss, and self-discovery.

Songs like "I Need U" and "Dope" strike a perfect balance between energetic beats and heartfelt lyrics, evoking a sense of nostalgia while urging listeners to embrace the struggles of youth. The music video for "I Need U" vividly portrays the chaos and emotional turmoil of adolescence, further enhancing the connection to the songs.The production quality and diverse musical styles also make this album shine.

From hip-hop to ballads, each track showcases the members' versatility and growth as artists. The inclusion of unique soundscapes and thoughtful instrumentation creates an immersive experience, allowing listeners to journey through the narrative of youth. Moreover, the album’s artwork and concept resonate deeply with the themes of fleeting moments and memories. It reminds us to cherish every experience, no matter how small. BTS's ability to convey such profound emotions through their music is what makes "The Most Beautiful Moment in Life, Pt. 1" not just an album, but a cherished soundtrack to my life. This collection of songs continues to inspire and uplift me, making it a timeless favorite.

As the 2025 reunion approaches, I can’t help but feel a mix of excitement, nostalgia, and hope. BTS has been such a big part of my life, and knowing that they’ll come together again after their individual journeys feels like a dream come true. Over the years, each member has grown in incredible ways, exploring solo projects and discovering new passions. While it’s been inspiring to see their personal growth, there’s something magical about BTS as a group that is truly irreplaceable.The thought of seeing RM, Jin, Suga, J-Hope, Jimin, V, and Jungkook on stage together again fills my heart with so much joy.
